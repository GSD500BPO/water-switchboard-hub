### El Paso's Water Story: Hardness and Salinity

The heart of El Paso's water comes from two primary sources: the Hueco Bolson aquifer and the Rio Grande. While these sources are vital, they also carry a high concentration of dissolved minerals like calcium and magnesium, leading to what's commonly known as "hard water."
