### Orlando's Water Reality: What Are You Drinking and Bathing In?

Orlando's water supply primarily comes from groundwater, which, while generally safe, presents a unique set of aesthetic and functional challenges:

*   **Elevated Hardness:** High mineral content (calcium and magnesium) leads to limescale buildup on fixtures and appliances, dry skin and hair, and reduced soap lathering. It’s a costly problem, forcing appliances to work harder and reducing their lifespan.
*   **Sulfur Odor:** Often described as a "rotten egg" smell, hydrogen sulfide gas is a common nuisance in groundwater. While not typically a health concern, it's unpleasant and can make tap water unpalatable.
*   **Iron Staining:** Iron in the water can lead to reddish-brown stains on sinks, toilets, laundry, and dishes, making cleaning a constant battle.

These issues are why many Orlando residents seek professional solutions, and this is precisely where **Water Filtration System** (RainSoft Orlando) steps in.
