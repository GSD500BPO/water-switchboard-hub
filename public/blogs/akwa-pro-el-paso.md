The naturally occurring minerals and salts in El Paso's water supply present a unique set of problems. Hard water can lead to limescale buildup in pipes and appliances, reducing their efficiency and lifespan. It can also make soap less effective, leaving residue on dishes, clothes, and even skin. For businesses, hard water can impact equipment, product quality, and operational costs.
