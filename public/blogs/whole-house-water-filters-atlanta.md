## Clearing the Air (and Water) in Atlanta: A Look at Whole House Water Filters Atlanta

Atlanta, a vibrant metropolis, draws its drinking water primarily from the Chattahoochee River. The good news for residents is that, by nature, this water is relatively soft. However, as is common with municipal water supplies across the country, the treatment process designed to make water safe often introduces its own set of challenges. Specifically, Atlanta's water can contain chlorine disinfection byproducts (DBPs), which, while not immediately harmful, can impact taste, odor, and raise long-term health questions for some consumers. This is precisely why many Atlantans are increasingly turning to professional water treatment companies to enhance their home water quality.
