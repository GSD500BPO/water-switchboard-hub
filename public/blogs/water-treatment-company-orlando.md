### Frequently Asked Questions About Orlando Water & Treatment

**Q: Why is Orlando's water so hard, and how does Blue Ribbon address it?**
A: Orlando's water supply comes from groundwater, which flows through underground limestone. As it travels, it picks up minerals like calcium and magnesium, making it "hard." Blue Ribbon Water Technology directly addresses this with **water softener installation**, which removes these hardness minerals, preventing scale buildup and improving water quality for daily use.

**Q: Can Blue Ribbon Water Technology really get rid of the rotten egg smell in my water?**
A: Yes, the "rotten egg" smell is typically caused by hydrogen sulfide gas, often associated with sulfur. Blue Ribbon Water Technology utilizes **whole house filtration** systems specifically designed to oxidize and remove sulfur, providing odor-free water throughout your entire home.

**Q: How do I know if I need water treatment in Orlando?**
A: Common signs include scale buildup on fixtures, stained laundry, a rotten egg smell, metallic tastes, or visible particles in your water. The best way to know for sure is through a professional **water test**, which Blue Ribbon Water Technology offers to accurately identify your specific water quality concerns.
