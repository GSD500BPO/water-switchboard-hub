### Miami's Water Woes: A Closer Look

The Biscayne Aquifer, while a vital resource, naturally contains a high concentration of dissolved minerals, predominantly calcium and magnesium. This is the root cause of what’s commonly known as "hard water" in Miami. The effects are visible everywhere: cloudy glassware, stubborn soap scum on showers and sinks, and even reduced efficiency and lifespan of water-using appliances like dishwashers and water heaters due to limescale accumulation.
