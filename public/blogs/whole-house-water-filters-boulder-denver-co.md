### Navigating Denver's Water Realities Beyond Softness

While Denver's water is naturally on the softer side, making traditional water softeners less of a universal necessity than in other regions, other factors can still significantly impact its quality at the tap:

*   **Aging Infrastructure:** Miles of pipes, some decades old, carry water from treatment plants to homes. Over time, these pipes can leach minerals, sediment, and even lead (in older homes with lead service lines), affecting water purity, taste, and smell.
*   **Seasonal Runoff:** As snow melts and rains fall, agricultural and urban runoff can introduce contaminants like pesticides, herbicides, and excess nutrients into surface water sources, which then require robust treatment by municipal plants. While city treatment is effective, some residents prefer an additional layer of protection.
