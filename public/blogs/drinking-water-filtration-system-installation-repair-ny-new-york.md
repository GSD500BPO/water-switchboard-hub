### The NYC Water Paradox: Pristine Source, Local Concerns

While the journey from upstate mountains to your tap begins with high-quality water, the final leg can be fraught with potential issues. Lead, a neurotoxin, can leach into drinking water from lead service lines, lead-containing solder in plumbing, or brass fixtures in older buildings. Even if the city's main lines are lead-free, your building's internal plumbing might not be. For homeowners and businesses in New York, understanding this risk is the first step toward proactive protection. Filtration isn't just a luxury; for many, it's a necessary safeguard against unseen contaminants.
