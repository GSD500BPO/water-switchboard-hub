### Frequently Asked Questions About Water Treatment in San Antonio

**Q: What exactly makes San Antonio water so hard, and how does Water Filtration help?**
A: San Antonio's water primarily comes from the Edwards Aquifer, an underground limestone formation. As water flows through this natural rock, it picks up dissolved minerals like calcium and magnesium, leading to its characteristic hardness (15-20 grains per gallon). Water Filtration directly addresses this with **water softener installation**, which removes these hardness minerals through an ion-exchange process, preventing scale buildup and improving water quality throughout your home.
