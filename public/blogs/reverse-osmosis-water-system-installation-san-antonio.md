### The RO Advantage for San Antonio Households

An RO system works by pushing water through a semi-permeable membrane, effectively filtering out microscopic impurities, dissolved solids, heavy metals, and the very minerals that contribute to San Antonio's significant water hardness. The result? Water that is remarkably pure, tastes better, and is gentler on your plumbing and appliances.
