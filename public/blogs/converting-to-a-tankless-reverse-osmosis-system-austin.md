### Understanding Austin's Water and Why Treatment Matters

The moderate hardness of Austin's water supply means it contains higher levels of dissolved minerals like calcium and magnesium. Over time, these minerals can lead to a host of problems: scale accumulating in pipes and appliances (reducing efficiency and lifespan), dry skin and hair after showering, and residue on dishes and fixtures. The seasonal changes, often influenced by rainfall and river flow, can also impact water clarity, taste, and odor throughout the year.
