### The Tampa Water Equation: Hardness + Chloramine

Let's break down Tampa's typical water profile. **Hardness** refers to the concentration of dissolved minerals like calcium and magnesium. In Tampa, these levels can lead to scale buildup on fixtures, inside pipes, and within water-using appliances, reducing efficiency and potentially shortening their lifespan. It can also make soaps less effective, leaving skin feeling dry and dishes spotty.

**Chloramine**, a disinfectant used by many municipalities, is another common concern. While effective at killing harmful bacteria, chloramine can contribute to an unpleasant taste and odor in drinking water, and some individuals may prefer to remove it for health or aesthetic reasons.
