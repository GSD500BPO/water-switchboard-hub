### The Miami Water Problem and the RO Solution

Let's break down why Reverse Osmosis is particularly relevant for Miami residents:

*   **Chlorine:** While essential for municipal disinfection, chlorine leaves an unmistakable taste and smell in tap water. RO systems effectively strip out chlorine, providing a cleaner, more pleasant drinking experience.
*   **Limestone Minerals:** The Biscayne Aquifer is naturally rich in calcium and magnesium – the culprits behind hard water. These minerals cause scale buildup in pipes and appliances, leave spots on dishes, and can even dry out skin and hair. Reverse Osmosis physically filters out these dissolved solids, delivering softer, purer water.
*   **Algae Byproducts:** Occasionally, seasonal algae blooms in surface water sources can contribute to earthy or musty tastes and odors in tap water. RO systems are highly effective at removing these microscopic organic compounds, ensuring a consistent quality regardless of external factors.
