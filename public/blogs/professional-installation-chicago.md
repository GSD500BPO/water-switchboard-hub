### The Chicago Water Challenge and the RO Solution

Lake Michigan provides a generally clean source, but the journey to your tap can be fraught with potential issues. Lead, a neurotoxin, is one of the most pressing. Standard municipal treatment processes don't always address lead pipes on private property. This is why many residents look to point-of-use or whole-home filtration.

Reverse Osmosis (RO) systems are widely recognized as one of the most effective methods for removing a broad spectrum of contaminants, including heavy metals like lead, as well as chlorine, fluoride, nitrates, and various dissolved solids. By forcing water through a semi-permeable membrane, RO provides exceptionally clean and great-tasting water directly from your tap.
