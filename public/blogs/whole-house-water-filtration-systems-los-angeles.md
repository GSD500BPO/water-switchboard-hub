Sourced from a complex network including the distant Colorado River and Northern California, LA's water travels far and carries a distinct profile – notably, a high mineral content that can impact everything from the taste of your morning coffee to the longevity of your appliances. For homeowners seeking to take control of their indoor water quality, understanding local solutions is key. This manifests as stubborn scale buildup on fixtures, cloudy spots on dishes, dry skin after showering, and can even shorten the lifespan of water-using appliances like dishwashers and water heaters. While the city's water is deemed safe to drink by regulatory standards, many residents desire an elevated level of clarity, taste, and protection for their homes.
