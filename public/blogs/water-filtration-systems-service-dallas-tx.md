Understanding your local water is the first step toward better water quality. In Dallas, water from area reservoirs is typically characterized by **moderate hardness**, often ranging from **8 to 12 grains per gallon (gpg)**. For many, this translates to common household nuisances like limescale buildup on fixtures and appliances, decreased soap lather, and even stiffer laundry.
