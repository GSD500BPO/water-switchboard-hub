While the water itself is safe to drink, the dissolved calcium and magnesium minerals cause the "hardness" that impacts daily life. Think about spotting on your dishes after washing, the extra effort needed to scrub soap scum from showers, or the reduced lifespan of water-using appliances like coffee makers and water heaters. These are all direct consequences of San Antonio's hard water.
