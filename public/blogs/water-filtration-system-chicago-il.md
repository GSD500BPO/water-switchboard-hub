While lead often takes center stage, other water quality issues impact daily life. Hard water, characterized by high mineral content, is common even in treated municipal supplies. It can lead to limescale buildup on fixtures, reduce the efficiency of water-using appliances, and leave skin and hair feeling dry. **Water Softener Installation** is a core service offered by Water Filtration System Chicago, IL, designed to mitigate these effects, extending the life of plumbing and appliances while improving personal comfort.
