### The Hard Truth About Salt Lake City's Water

The majestic Wasatch Front, with its snow-capped peaks and underground aquifers, is the primary source of Salt Lake City's water. As this water flows over and through geological formations, it naturally absorbs minerals like calcium and magnesium. This process results in what's known as "hard water."
