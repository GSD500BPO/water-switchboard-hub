### Beyond Softness: Understanding Sacramento's Water Challenges

While Sacramento's natural water sources provide a generally soft supply, the vast agricultural landscape surrounding the city introduces a dynamic element to water quality. Seasonal changes can bring increased agricultural runoff into the river systems. This runoff can carry various contaminants, including sediment, pesticides, herbicides, and other dissolved solids, even if the water remains chemically soft. For homeowners, this can translate to concerns about taste, odor, and the potential long-term effects of consuming these trace elements. Many Sacramento residents seek peace of mind, desiring water that is not just soft, but also free from these agricultural byproducts and other common urban pollutants like chlorine.
