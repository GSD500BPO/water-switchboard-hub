### Phoenix's Unique Water Landscape

Phoenix draws its water primarily from the Salt and Verde Rivers, supplemented by the Colorado River via the Central Arizona Project. While these sources provide essential water to the bustling metropolis, the desert environment and geological factors often lead to **elevated Total Dissolved Solids (TDS)**. This means Phoenix residents frequently contend with hard water – water high in minerals like calcium and magnesium – which can cause scale buildup in pipes and appliances, dry skin and hair, and even affect the taste of drinking water.
