### Understanding Houston's Unique Water Profile

The diverse sources feeding Houston's water supply contribute to its unique characteristics. The city's water can be moderately hard in some areas and significantly harder in others, leading to a range of common household frustrations. Hard water is notorious for:

*   **Limescale buildup:** Clogging pipes, spotting dishes, and reducing the efficiency and lifespan of appliances like water heaters, dishwashers, and washing machines.
*   **Dry skin and dull hair:** Hard minerals can react with soaps and shampoos, leaving a residue that irritates skin and strips hair of its natural luster.
*   **Faded laundry:** Minerals can bind to fabric, making clothes look dingy and feel stiff.
