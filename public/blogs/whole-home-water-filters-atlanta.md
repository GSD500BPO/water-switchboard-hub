## Navigating Atlanta's Water: Understanding Your Options with Whole Home Water Filters (Culligan Atlanta)

Atlanta, a city known for its vibrant culture and lush landscapes, draws its primary water supply from the Chattahoochee River. These can affect water taste, odor, and for some residents, raise long-term health concerns, prompting many homeowners and businesses to seek advanced water treatment solutions.
