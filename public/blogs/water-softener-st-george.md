## Taming the Desert Hardness: Pace's Culligan and Water Treatment in St. George

St. George, Utah, is known for its stunning red rock landscapes, vibrant community, and abundant sunshine. Sourced primarily from southern Utah's underground aquifers, the water in St. George carries a high concentration of dissolved minerals like calcium and magnesium. This mineral-rich water can lead to a host of problems, from stubborn limescale buildup and inefficient appliances to dry skin and dull laundry.
