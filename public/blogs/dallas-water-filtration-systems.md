### Navigating Dallas's Water Landscape

Dallas water, sourced primarily from area reservoirs, comes with its own set of characteristics. Residents often contend with **moderately hard water**, typically ranging from 8 to 12 grains per gallon. While not unsafe, this level of hardness can lead to visible issues like mineral buildup on fixtures, scale accumulation in appliances, and less effective soap lathering. Beyond hardness, **seasonal fluctuations in chlorine levels** can impact taste and odor, making tap water less appealing. **Turbidity**, or cloudiness, can also vary, particularly after heavy rainfall or during maintenance.
