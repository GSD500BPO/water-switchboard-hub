### What Does "Moderately Hard" Water Mean for Your Salt Lake City Home?

A water hardness level of 12-15 GPG is significant. To put it in perspective, anything above 7 GPG is generally considered hard. This level of hardness means your water contains a higher concentration of dissolved minerals like calcium and magnesium. While not a health hazard, these minerals are the culprits behind:

*   **Unsightly Scale Buildup:** On faucets, showerheads, dishwashers, and inside pipes.
*   **Reduced Appliance Lifespan:** Mineral deposits can clog and damage water heaters, washing machines, and dishwashers, leading to costly repairs or early replacements.
*   **Ineffective Cleaning:** Soaps and detergents struggle to lather properly, leading to more product use and less effective cleaning for dishes, clothes, and even your body.
*   **Dry Skin and Hair:** Hard water can strip natural oils, leaving skin feeling dry and hair looking lackluster.
