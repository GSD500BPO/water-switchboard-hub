### Sacramento's Water Story: Beyond the Tap

While the city's municipal water generally meets safety standards, the impact of agricultural runoff from surrounding areas can be a legitimate concern. This runoff can carry pesticides, herbicides, nitrates, and other dissolved solids into the river systems, which, despite treatment, can sometimes manifest as subtle changes in water quality at the tap. For those relying on private wells in the surrounding areas, this issue can be even more pronounced. This fluctuating quality often drives the demand for in-home water treatment.
