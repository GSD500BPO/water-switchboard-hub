### Understanding Los Angeles' Unique Water Challenges

The journey of LA's water from its diverse sources means it picks up various minerals along the way, predominantly calcium and magnesium. These are the culprits behind the hard water experience. You might notice:
*   Unsightly white scale on faucets, showerheads, and dishware.
*   Reduced efficiency and lifespan of water-using appliances like water heaters and washing machines.
*   More soap and detergent needed to create lather, leading to higher utility bills.
*   Residue on skin and hair after showering, contributing to dryness.

These aren't just minor inconveniences; they represent real costs and impacts on daily life.
