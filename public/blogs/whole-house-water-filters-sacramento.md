### Sacramento's Unique Water Profile and Local Solutions

The "soft" nature of Sacramento's river water means you're less likely to experience the tell-tale scale buildup common in hard water regions. However, agricultural activities upstream and surrounding the city can sometimes lead to increased levels of sediments, pesticides, or other organic matter that can impact taste, odor, and overall water aesthetics. Beyond this, aging infrastructure can contribute to issues like chlorine byproducts or even trace metals in some older neighborhoods.
