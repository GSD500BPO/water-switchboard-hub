### Understanding Denver's Unique Water Profile

Denver Water delivers some of the best-tasting water in the nation, with a natural softness derived from its snowmelt origins. However, the infrastructure carrying this water to homes and businesses has seen decades of use. Over time, pipes can degrade, potentially leading to issues like sediment, chlorine taste, or even localized mineral buildup that might contradict the "naturally soft" label. Seasonal changes, especially during heavy snowmelt or rain, can also increase the presence of various organic compounds or turbidity in the water supply.
