**The Unique Challenges of Miami's Water**

Miami's water journey begins deep within the **Biscayne Aquifer**, a natural underground reservoir. While generally safe to drink, the aquifer's geology presents some common challenges for local households:

*   **Elevated Limestone Minerals:** This is the primary culprit behind Miami's notoriously "hard water." Limestone deposits lead to scale buildup on fixtures, inside pipes, and within water-using appliances like dishwashers and water heaters, reducing their efficiency and lifespan.
