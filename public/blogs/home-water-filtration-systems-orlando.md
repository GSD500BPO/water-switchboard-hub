**Understanding Orlando's Unique Water Challenges**

Before diving into solutions, it's vital to understand the problem. Orlando's groundwater, while generally safe, presents specific characteristics:

*   **Hardness:** High mineral content, primarily calcium and magnesium, leads to limescale buildup on faucets, showerheads, and inside pipes and appliances. This reduces efficiency and lifespan, and makes soap less effective.
*   **Sulfur Odor:** Often described as a "rotten egg" smell, this is caused by hydrogen sulfide gas present in groundwater. While typically not a health risk, it's highly unpleasant.
*   **Iron Staining:** Dissolved iron in the water can oxidize and leave reddish-brown stains on sinks, toilets, laundry, and even affect the taste of drinking water.

These issues are common across much of Central Florida, driving many residents to explore professional water treatment options.
