Phoenix, the Valley of the Sun, is known for its stunning desert landscapes and vibrant city life. Drawing its supply from the Salt and Verde Rivers and the mighty Colorado River, Phoenix’s water, combined with the arid climate and an extensive distribution network, frequently carries a higher mineral content. This isn't necessarily a health hazard, but it manifests in noticeable ways – from mineral buildup on fixtures and appliances to a distinct taste that many find unappealing.
