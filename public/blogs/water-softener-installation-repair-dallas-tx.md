### Understanding Dallas's Water Landscape

Before diving into how Harlen Johnson helps, it's crucial to understand what Dallas residents are up against. The city's water, primarily sourced from area reservoirs, is characterized by its moderate hardness, typically ranging between **8 and 12 grains per gallon (GPG)**. While not extreme, this level of hardness is more than enough to cause noticeable issues in homes and businesses. Think unsightly mineral buildup (scale) on fixtures and appliances, decreased efficiency of water heaters, dry skin and hair after showering, and dull, faded laundry.

Beyond hardness, Dallas water also experiences **seasonal fluctuations in chlorine levels**. While chlorine is essential for disinfecting public water supplies, its presence in tap water can lead to unpleasant tastes, odors, and can even contribute to drying out skin and hair.
