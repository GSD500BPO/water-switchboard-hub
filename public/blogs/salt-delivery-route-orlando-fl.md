**Understanding Orlando's Water Woes**

Let's break down why local residents often seek water treatment:

*   **Hardness:** High mineral content (calcium and magnesium) leads to limescale buildup on fixtures, inside pipes, and within appliances like water heaters and dishwashers. This not only reduces appliance efficiency and lifespan but also makes soaps and detergents less effective, leaving skin and hair feeling dry and dull.
*   **Sulfur Odor:** Often described as a "rotten egg" smell, this is caused by hydrogen sulfide gas dissolved in the water. While generally not harmful, it's certainly unpleasant and can make basic tasks like showering or washing dishes an ordeal.
*   **Iron Staining:** Elevated iron levels can cause reddish-brown stains on sinks, toilets, laundry, and even affect the taste of drinking water.
