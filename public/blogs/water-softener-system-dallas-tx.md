Dallas water, at 8-12 GPG, is considered moderately hard. Over time, this buildup reduces efficiency, increases energy costs, and can significantly shorten the lifespan of these valuable household items. Hard water also impacts the effectiveness of soaps and detergents and can leave skin and hair feeling dry and dull.
