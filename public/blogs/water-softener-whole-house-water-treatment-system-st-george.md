### Understanding St. George's Water Challenge

The geological makeup of Southern Utah means that as water travels through underground aquifers, it picks up significant concentrations of minerals like calcium and magnesium. These are the culprits behind "hard water." While not a health risk, hard water can wreak havoc on plumbing, heating elements in water heaters, and even the effectiveness of soaps and detergents. Over time, mineral buildup (limescale) can reduce appliance lifespan, increase energy bills, and leave skin and hair feeling dry.
